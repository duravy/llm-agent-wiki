Domain 5 covers the design and operation of long-running, multi-agent systems. It is the fifth and final domain of the [[series-overview|Claude Architect Certification Exam series]]. Topics focus on real-world failure modes in production agents — context loss, ambiguity, error propagation, codebase navigation, human oversight, and information provenance.
